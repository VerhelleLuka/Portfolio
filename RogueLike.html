Go Rock Yourself

A Roguelite where you can upgrade your weapons, kill hordes of enemies and rock everything in your path!.

In this personal project I am out to write clean and efficient code, as of writing this the game includes the following:

-Three different weapons! (Branching upgrades paths are WIP)
-A general Health system using events.
-Easy to navigate menu (shop is WIP)
-An automated attack system so you can focus on dodging and weaving your enemies!
-DOTween implementation

This project is really fun to work on, I am still developing it and having a blast doing so!
