Earwaxguy

A fun fast paced platformer, where you have to dig, swing and dash your way through a level, avoiding enemies and collecting cue tips as you go.

In this personal project I was out to both regain some knowledge and learn some new things, such as:

-Using physics based movement to propel your character through the levels
-Implementing a simple but functional pathing system for enemies
-Marching Squares algorithm for digging through wax
-Using the new unity input system to control your character


This project was really fun to work on, I learned a lot about the marching squares algorithm and it was a good refresher on Unity!
