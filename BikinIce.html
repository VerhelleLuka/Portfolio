Bikin' Ice

A fun, fast paced, couch co-op karting game where you have to collect coins and try to stop your opponents from getting them

In this school project I was in charge of implementing the following game mechanics:

  • Management of the coins and power ups
  • Powerup behavior and effects on players
  • Rising of water level
  • Jumping and Basic Driving

This school project was really fun to work on, and taught me a lot about driving mechanics, scriptable objects and the observer programming patern pattern, as well as coroutines.
